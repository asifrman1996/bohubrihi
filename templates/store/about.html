Our Story

Rooted in Bangladesh. Crafted with nature. Made for you.

Why Bohubrihi?

Bohubrihi Organics was born from a simple belief — that the finest skincare ingredients grow right here in Bangladesh. For generations, our grandmothers kept their skin radiant using neem, turmeric, rose water, and coconut oil. We set out to bottle that wisdom.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches, using certified organic ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. No parabens, no sulfates, no artificial fragrances — just pure nature working the way it always has.

We're proud to be a Bangladeshi brand making world-class organic skincare accessible to everyone, at honest prices.
